                                                <h2 class="h5">Summary</h2>
                                                <p>The course focuses on the development of real-word AI/ML products. It is intended for students who have acquired a theoretical background in AI/ML and are interested in applying that toward developing AI/ML-oriented products.</p>
                                    </div>
                                    <div class="mt-5">
                                                <h2 class="h5">Content</h2>
                                            <div class="collapse collapse-partial" id="course-content">
                                                <p><p>AI is set to transform several industry sectors, and there is high demand for AI product managers. AI Product Management (AIPM) is a complex role that requires an understanding of both AI and product management. This course will enable students to identify opportunities for developing new AI products, understand when they should use AI in an existing product/process, manage the development of AI products, and launch AI products successfully. The lectures will introduce general product management to the students, and the guest lectures, by leading figures in AI industries, explain how the general product management skills are applied to the development and delivery of AI products.</p> <p>&#160;</p> <p><strong>Module 1: Introduction to AI Product Management (AIPM)</strong></p> <ul>
<li>The rise of AIPM: what is it and why are AI product managers becoming essential?</li>
<li>Core challenges: What makes AIPM uniquely complex? </li>
<li>Success in AIPM: What defines a successful AI product/project?</li>
</ul> <p><strong>Module 2: AI Product Discovery</strong></p> <ul>
<li>Identify the problem clearly: Understand customer needs, user profiles, value proposition, and competitor landscape.</li>
<li>Address critical risks early: Analyze and test risks related to value, usability, feasibility, and viability.<strong><br></strong></li>
<li>Build and test the right MVP: Identify the problem, prioritize assumptions, set success criteria, choose MVP type, deliver, and iterate. </li>
<li>Refining AI Product Strategy: Use insights from discovery to re-shape the vision, startegy, define the roadmap, and document the product journey (PRD). </li>
</ul> <p><strong>Module 3: AI Product Development</strong></p> <ul>
<li>Master agile and iterative development </li>
<li>Align design, testing, and development of AI systems</li>
<li>Manage data readiness and feasibility</li>
<li>Foster team dynamics and ethical development practices </li>
<li>Communicate effectively with stakeholders</li>
</ul> <p><strong>Module 4: AI Product Delivery</strong></p> <ul>
<li>Planning and executing a successful AI product launch</li>
<li>Market AI capabilities effectively </li>
<li>Ensure monitoring, user adoption, and continuous improvement</li>
<li>Address governance, trust, and responsible AI</li>
</ul> <ul>
</ul></p>

                                                <h2 class="h5">Learning Prerequisites</h2>
                                                <h2 class="h6">Required courses</h2>
                                                <p><p>CS-233 Introduction to machine learning or CS-433 Machinie learning or equivalent course on the basics of machine learning and deep learning</p></p>
                                                <h2 class="h6">Important concepts to start the course</h2>
                                                <p><ul>
<li>Python programming</li>
<li>Bascis of deep learning and machine learning</li>
<li>Basics of probability and statistics</li>
</ul></p>
                                    </div>
                                    <div class="mt-5">
                                                <h2 class="h5">Learning Outcomes</h2>
                                                <p>By the end of the course, the student must be able to:<br></p>
                                                <p><ul><li>and understand opportunities for an AI product or using AI within an existing product</li><li>the development of AI features</li><li>Launch AI products successfully</li></ul></p>
                                    </div>
                                    <div class="mt-5">
                                                <h2 class="h5">Transversal skills</h2>
                                                <p><ul><li>Demonstrate the capacity for critical thinking</li><li>Evaluate one's own performance in the team, receive and respond appropriately to feedback.</li><li>Communicate effectively, being understood, including across different languages and cultures.</li><li>Set objectives and design an action plan to reach those objectives.</li><li>Chair a meeting to achieve a particular agenda, maximising participation.</li><li>Resolve conflicts in ways that are productive for the task and the people concerned.</li><li>Make an oral presentation.</li><li>Take account of the social and human dimensions of the engineering profession.</li></ul></p>
                                    </div>
